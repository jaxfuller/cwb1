Leadville Travel Guide


Leadville, Colorado

Leadville, Colorado is a small former mining town in central Colorado. At an elevation of over 10,000 ft, Leadville sits right at the base of the Sawatch mountain range and Mt. Elbert, the tallest mountain in the Colorado. The geographic location makes Leadville a hotspot for outdoor recreation year round. From historic locations, crystal clear streams, luscious forrest in the summer, and deep powder snow in the winter, anyone can find something to do!
